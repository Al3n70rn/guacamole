    Main PartitionedRegions constructor: given some regions and loci, assign the loci to Spark partitions, and then partition the regions according to which partitions' loci they overlap.

    Main PartitionedRegions constructor: given some regions and loci, assign the loci to Spark partitions, and then partition the regions according to which partitions' loci they overlap.

    Computes a LociPartitioning and delegates to the other constructor below.

    R

    ReferenceRegion type.

    regionRDDs

    RDDs of regions to partition.

    loci

    Genomic loci to operate on; these will be split among Spark partitions and coupled with all regions from regionRDDs that overlap them (module the half-window described below).

    args

    Parameters dictating how loci should be partitioned.

    halfWindowSize

    A region is considered to overlap a partition's loci if it passes within this distance of any of them, in which case a copy of that region will be sent to that partition (possibly among others).
